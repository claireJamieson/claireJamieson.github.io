Claire Jamieson

I have 35 years of teaching experience and was Head of Department for Computer Science at secondary level. I now provide private tuition in Maths for Eleven Plus and GCSE, and also help pupils prepare for school entrance exams.

I have had three pupils achieve ‘top five’ status (ie top five individual pupils in the country, not merely top 5%) at GCSE level — two for ICT and one for BCS (Business Communication Systems). A very large majority of my pupils at GCSE level in ICT and BCS have achieved grade A or A*, and this from pupils of mixed abilities. Similarly a very large majority of my pupils at A-level Computing have gained grade A or B. Of my A-level pupils who have gone on to study Computing and related subjects at university, many have gained first class honours and some have been awarded ‘best in year’ and ‘best overall’ prizes at UCL and Imperial College.

I have the ability to communicate effectively to pupils of all abilities. I make a point of ensuring that pupils always have the understanding they need, and will keep working at a problem, tackling it in different ways as necessary, until I know that they have grasped it. I have been very successful in helping pupils to gain the GCSE grade they aspire to, particularly pupils who find maths really difficult.

I am married with three grown-up children.
